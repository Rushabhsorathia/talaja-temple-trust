# Devotee / User Manual — Talaja Temple Trust Portal

## Browse
- **Home:** services, latest news, trustees.
- **Temple Info:** darshan/aarti/pooja timings, festival calendar, map.
- **Gallery:** photos & videos. **News:** updates & events.
- **Live Darshan:** watch live aarti (when streaming).

## Language
- Switch English / Gujarati (ગુ) from the top utility bar — remembered across pages.

## Donate
1. Click **Donate**.
2. Choose an amount (preset chip or custom).
3. Pick a category; tick **80G** if you need a tax receipt (enter PAN).
4. Fill name/mobile/email; optionally stay anonymous.
5. Pay securely (Razorpay). On success → **download your receipt**.
- Login optional; logged-in donors see history at **My Donations**.

## Book a Room / Hall
1. **Bookings** tab → Rooms.
2. Pick dates → **Check Availability**.
3. Choose room type, guests, payment mode (Pay at Temple or Online).
4. Confirm → get a booking number.
- Meeting Halls: pick hall, date, time, attendees → confirm.
- View your bookings at **My Bookings** (login required).

## Shop
1. Browse **Shop** → **Add to Cart**.
2. **Cart** → fill name/mobile/address → **Place Order**.
3. Track in **My Orders**.

## Account
- **Login via OTP:** enter mobile → receive OTP → verify. New devotees enter name.
- **Profile:** update name, mobile, PAN (for 80G), address, change password.

## Contact & Feedback
- **Contact Us:** send suggestion/feedback/complaint with optional rating.
