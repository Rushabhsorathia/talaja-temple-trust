# M2-T9 — Contact, FAQs, Downloads

- **Type:** Story
- **Priority:** Medium
- **Story Points:** 3
- **Milestone:** M2 – Public CMS Site
- **Stack:** Laravel + Vue (Inertia)

## User Story
> As a visitor, I want to contact the trust, read FAQs and download publications.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Contact page (form -> email + store), Google Map, address, phone, email.
- [ ] FAQ accordion (category grouped, EN/GU).
- [ ] Downloads/Publications (PDF list with category).
- [ ] Form spam protection (honeypot/reCAPTCHA).

## Definition of Done
Submissions stored & emailed; downloads work.
