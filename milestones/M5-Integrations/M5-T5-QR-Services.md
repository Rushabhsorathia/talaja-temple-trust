# M5-T5 — Dynamic UPI QR Service

- **Type:** Story
- **Priority:** Medium
- **Story Points:** 3
- **Milestone:** M5 – Integrations
- **Stack:** Laravel + QR library

## User Story
> As a donor, I want a QR that pre-fills my donation amount.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Generate dynamic UPI deep-link QR (amount, note).
- [ ] Display in donate modal.
- [ ] Optional: status poll via Razorpay.

## Definition of Done
QR opens UPI app with prefilled amount.
