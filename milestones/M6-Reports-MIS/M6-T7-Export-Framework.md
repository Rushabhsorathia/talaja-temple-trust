# M6-T7 — Unified Export Framework (CSV/Excel/PDF)

- **Type:** Story
- **Priority:** High
- **Story Points:** 3
- **Milestone:** M6 – Reports & MIS

## User Story
> As a user, I want consistent export across all reports & lists.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Shared export service (Maatwebsite Excel + DomPDF).
- [ ] Standardised headers/footers/branding.
- [ ] Large-dataset queued export.

## Definition of Done
Any list/report exportable with one click.
