# M6-T4 — Financial Reports

- **Type:** Story
- **Priority:** High
- **Story Points:** 5
- **Milestone:** M6 – Reports & MIS

## User Story
> As finance, I want receipts/payments/reconciliation reports.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Receipts & payments statement.
- [ ] Bank reconciliation report.
- [ ] P&L summary.
- [ ] Ledger / cash book.
- [ ] Period comparison.

## Definition of Done
Reports reviewed and approved by accountant.
