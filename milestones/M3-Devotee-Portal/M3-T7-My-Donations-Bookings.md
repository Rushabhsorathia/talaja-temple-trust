# M3-T7 — My Donations & My Bookings

- **Type:** Story
- **Priority:** High
- **Story Points:** 3
- **Milestone:** M3 – Devotee Portal
- **Stack:** Laravel + Vue

## User Story
> As a logged-in devotee, I want to view my donation & booking history and download receipts.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] My Donations list (date, amount, category, 80G status, receipt PDF).
- [ ] My Bookings list (status, dates, voucher, cancel).
- [ ] Filter by date/type; export CSV.
- [ ] Annual donation summary (for tax filing).

## Definition of Done
History accurate; downloads work.
