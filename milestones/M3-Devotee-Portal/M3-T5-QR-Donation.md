# M3-T5 — QR-Based UPI Donation

- **Type:** Story
- **Priority:** Medium
- **Story Points:** 3
- **Milestone:** M3 – Devotee Portal
- **Stack:** Laravel + Vue

## User Story
> As a donor without a card, I want to scan a UPI QR and donate.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Dynamic UPI QR (BharatQR/Razorpay) encoding amount + note.
- [ ] Donate modal shows QR + bank details (like reference site).
- [ ] Payment status polling / webhook.
- [ ] Manual "I have paid" confirmation fallback with admin verify.

## Definition of Done
QR scans to UPI app with prefilled amount.
